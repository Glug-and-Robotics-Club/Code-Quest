Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Code Quest?

Code Quest is a three-round coding and aptitude challenge where students can explore their skills and solve numerous coding problems.

How do I get there?

It is happening in Swami Vivekananda block at School of CSE,REVA University

What if I don't have a team?

If you dont have a team then you can participate solo.

What types of Questions will be asked ?

The questions in this context will mostly relate to aptitude, basic knowledge of programming languages, and data structures.

I am not a CSE student; can I participate?

Yes, participants from any discipline are welcome to join Code Quest, as it is a multidisciplinary event.

Is there any prize money for this event?

Yes, the top 3 teams will receive a cash prize, and all the top contestants will get premium goodies. Additionally, all participants will receive goodies.

Who can attend?

You are eligible to attend if you're a college student.

What about accomodation?

As each round of the contest will be held during college hours, no accommodation will be provided.

Have some other questions?
